3. About Gems

How Gems Work in Tipsy

In Tipsy, Gems are used throughout the app. Both are visible on your profile page.

The image above shows two types of Gems: Red Gems and Blue Gems.

They work the same way, but differ in how you get them and how long they last.

  • Red Gems never expire and can be earned or purchased.
  • Blue Gems are exclusive to subscribers and refresh monthly based on your subscription plan.

Both Gems can be used across Tipsy wherever Gems are required.

When spending Gems, Blue Gems are deducted first, followed by Red Gems.
